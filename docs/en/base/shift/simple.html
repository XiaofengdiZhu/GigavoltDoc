Complex Elements ‚Äã

SeeÔºö

  • Digital to Analog And Analog to Digital Converter - Changes to 4 combiners and 4 splitters.
  • Memory Bank - The row and column limits are raised to 232-1.
  • Truth Table Circuit - Besides being able to output voltages based on specified inputs like the classic type, it can also perform expression calculations.
